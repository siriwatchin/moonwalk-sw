◗ Moon Walk WHOOP for your walking aid. metric mockup · mock data
Two headline widgets adapted to gait — a Gait Profile radar (fair, baseline-relative) and the Moon Walk Score gauge.

Gait Profile

Each axis = a gait dimension, plotted as % of that walker's own baseline (100 = your normal). Compares walkers fairly — shape vs their own normal, not raw ability.

Moon Walk Score — all walkers

Each walker's score today (0–100), relative to their own baseline — a fair number for everyone.

⚕ MEDICAL CLAIM SAFETY — a wellness awareness cue, not a medical assessment. Scores are relative to each user's own baseline, never a population norm.
